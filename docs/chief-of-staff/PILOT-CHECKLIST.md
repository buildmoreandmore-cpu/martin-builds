# AI Chief of Staff — Pilot Onboarding Checklist

## Pre-Call

- [ ] Prospect filled out interest form or commented "CHIEFOFSTAFF"
- [ ] Research their business (website, reviews, team size, revenue estimate)
- [ ] Check their Google reviews (volume, recency, rating)
- [ ] Check their website response time (do they have a contact form? Live chat?)
- [ ] Identify 3 specific pain points to bring up on the call

## Discovery Call (30 min)

### Questions to Ask
1. How many leads/inquiries do you get per week?
2. How fast do you typically respond to new leads?
3. What's your no-show rate?
4. Who handles your scheduling right now?
5. How do you follow up with leads who don't book?
6. Do you ask for Google reviews? How?
7. What does your morning look like? (Trying to find the "drowning in admin" moment)
8. Have you tried hiring for this role? What happened?
9. What tools do you currently use? (Email, calendar, CRM, scheduling)
10. If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing, what would it be?

### What to Listen For
- "I know I should follow up but I just don't have time"
- "We lose leads because we don't respond fast enough"
- "I tried hiring someone but they quit / it didn't work out"
- "My calendar has gaps but I don't know how to fill them"
- "I don't have time to ask for reviews"

### Close
- "Based on what you've told me, here's exactly what the Chief of Staff would do for you..."
- Map their pain points to specific layers
- Offer the pilot: $1,500/month for 60 days, full system
- If they need to think about it: "I'll send you a summary of everything we discussed"

## Post-Call (Same Day)

- [ ] Send recap email with specific pain points + how each layer solves them
- [ ] Include 2-3 industry-specific examples
- [ ] Include pilot terms (60 days, $1,500/mo, money-back)
- [ ] Add to CRM/pipeline

## If They Say Yes — Onboarding

### Day 1: Account Setup
- [ ] Stripe subscription created ($1,500/mo pilot)
- [ ] Client record in Supabase
- [ ] Bot assigned from pool + renamed
- [ ] Welcome email sent

### Day 1-2: Tool Connections
- [ ] Gmail/Outlook connected via Composio OAuth
- [ ] Google Calendar connected
- [ ] CRM connected (if applicable)
- [ ] Stripe/Square connected (if applicable)
- [ ] Verify all connections are active

### Day 2-3: Business Configuration
- [ ] Industry playbook selected + customized
- [ ] Email response templates reviewed + approved by owner
- [ ] Follow-up sequence timing configured
- [ ] Post-service sequence configured
- [ ] Review request template + Google review link set
- [ ] Briefing time + channel configured
- [ ] Blackout hours set (no messages before X AM / after X PM)
- [ ] Owner communication preferences set (Telegram/WhatsApp/email)

### Day 3-4: Data Import
- [ ] Import existing contacts/leads (CSV or CRM sync)
- [ ] Import recent email history (last 30 days for training)
- [ ] Import upcoming calendar events
- [ ] Set up lead sources (forms, email patterns, etc.)

### Day 4-5: Shadow Mode
- [ ] AI processes emails but DRAFTS only (doesn't send)
- [ ] Owner reviews 20+ draft responses
- [ ] Accuracy rate check (target: 90%+ correct classification)
- [ ] Template adjustments based on feedback
- [ ] Calendar reminders tested with owner's phone
- [ ] Daily briefing format approved

### Day 5-7: Soft Launch
- [ ] Email auto-send ON for: new leads, reschedules, confirmations
- [ ] Email auto-send OFF for: complaints, large quotes (owner approves)
- [ ] Calendar reminders ON
- [ ] Follow-up sequences ON
- [ ] Daily briefing ON
- [ ] Owner checks in daily for first week

### Day 7-14: Full Launch
- [ ] Post-service automation ON
- [ ] Review requests ON
- [ ] Gap-filling ON
- [ ] All layers active
- [ ] Weekly check-in call

### Day 14: First Review
- [ ] Review metrics: response time, follow-up rate, no-show rate
- [ ] Owner feedback on quality
- [ ] Adjustments to templates/timing
- [ ] Document early wins for case study

### Day 30: Pilot Review
- [ ] Full metrics report
- [ ] ROI calculation (revenue gained + time saved vs. cost)
- [ ] Case study draft (with owner's permission)
- [ ] Conversion to full pricing or continue pilot
- [ ] Expand scope if appropriate

### Day 60: Pilot End
- [ ] Final metrics comparison (before vs. after)
- [ ] Convert to standard pricing ($2,000-$5,000/mo)
- [ ] Testimonial request
- [ ] Referral ask

## Success Criteria (Prove Value in 60 Days)

| Metric | Before | Target |
|--------|--------|--------|
| Lead response time | 4-24 hours | < 5 minutes |
| Follow-up completion rate | 20-30% | 100% |
| No-show rate | 10-20% | < 5% |
| Reviews requested/month | 0-5 | 20+ |
| Owner admin hours/week | 15-25 | < 5 |
| Calendar gaps filled | 0% | 50%+ |

## Red Flags (Don't Pilot With)

- Business under $500K/year (can't afford it long-term)
- Owner who won't let AI email their customers (trust issue — not ready)
- No Gmail/Google Calendar (Outlook works but adds complexity)
- Business with <5 leads/week (not enough volume to show results)
- Owner who wants to review every single email before it sends (defeats the purpose)
