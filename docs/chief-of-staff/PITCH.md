## What it actually does

### Before you wake up, it's already working.

**Emails**: Every email that hits your inbox gets read, sorted, and handled. New lead? Responded to in under 5 minutes — even at 2 AM. Reschedule request? Already checked your calendar and sent options. Complaint? Flagged to you immediately with a draft response.

**Calendar**: Your schedule is optimized automatically. Customers get reminders so they actually show up. When someone cancels, the system reaches out to fill the gap before you even know it's empty.

**Follow-ups**: Every single lead gets followed up with. Not once — multiple times, with different messages, until they book or say no. No more "I forgot to call them back."

**After the job**: Thank-you messages go out automatically. Review requests at the right time. Upsell offers for maintenance plans or return visits. Your past customers keep coming back.

**Daily briefing**: One message at the end of every day. Everything that happened, everything that's coming tomorrow, anything that needs your attention. Read one message. Know everything.

## FAQ

**Will it sound like a robot?**
No. We train it on YOUR voice — how you talk to customers, your tone, your style. Customers won't know the difference.

**What if it makes a mistake?**
Complaints and sensitive situations are always flagged for your approval before any response goes out. You stay in control of what matters.

**Can I turn it off?**
Anytime. But you won't want to.

**What about my existing CRM/tools?**
We connect to what you already use. Gmail, Outlook, Google Calendar, Stripe, Square, HubSpot, Salesforce — we work with all of them.

**How is this different from a chatbot?**
A chatbot sits on your website and waits. The Chief of Staff reads your email, manages your calendar, follows up with leads, asks for reviews, and sends you a briefing. It runs your business operations, not just your website.
